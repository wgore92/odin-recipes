Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These amazing bakery style chocolate chip cookies are to die for!

They are super easy to bake with ingredients that you can easily find in your home grocery store.

Follow along and let me know what your thoughts are!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, at cool room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups lightly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In the bowl of an electric mixer, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy, about 2 minutes. Add the vanilla and eggs. Gradually beat in the flour mixture. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  4. If time permits, wrap dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 24 hours but no more than 72 hours.
  5. Let dough sit at room temperature until soft enough to scoop.
  6. Divide dough into 3 table-spoon sized balls using a large cookie scoop and drop onto prepared baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 11-13 minutes, or unitl golden brown. Cool for 5 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
  8. The cookies can be stored in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
