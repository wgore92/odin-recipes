Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These amazing bakery style chocolate chip cookies are to die for! They are super easy to bake with ingredients that you can easily find in your home grocery store. Follow along and let me know what your thoughts are!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, at cool room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups lightly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
